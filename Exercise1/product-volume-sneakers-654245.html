Description

Volume sole lace up sneakers. Made from 98% recycled material. Combined design. Designed to provide comfort with a volume sole for greater cushioning.

Materials

  • Insole: 100% thermoplastic rubber.

Washing Instructions

Remove laces and insoles.
Place in a mesh bag.
Wash in a gentle, cold water cycle with a slow or no-spin setting at no higher than 30°.
